Budgeting

Budgeting is the process of creating a plan to spend your money. This spending plan is called a budget. Creating this spending plan allows you to determine in advance whether you will have enough money to do the things you need to do or would like to do.

Why Budget?

Budgeting helps you:

  • Manage your money effectively
  • Save for future goals
  • Prevent unnecessary debt
  • Prepare for emergencies

Saving

Saving money involves setting aside a portion of your income for future use. This can help you achieve financial goals, prepare for emergencies, and avoid debt.

Tips for Saving

  • Set specific savings goals
  • Create a savings plan
  • Automate your savings
  • Reduce unnecessary expenses

Investing

Investing is the act of allocating resources, usually money, with the expectation of generating an income or profit. It can involve buying assets like stocks, bonds, real estate, or starting a business.

Types of Investments

  • Stocks: Ownership in a company
  • Bonds: Loans to a company or government
  • Real Estate: Property investments
  • Mutual Funds: Pooled investments
